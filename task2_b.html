Titanic Data Cleaning and EDA

Data Cleaning

Handling missing values:

  • Age: filled with mean age (29.7)
  • Embarked: filled with most frequent value (Southampton)

Encoding categorical variables:

  • Sex: male=0, female=1
  • Embarked: Southampton=0, Cherbourg=1, Queenstown=2

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Variable Relationships

Variable 1 Variable 2 Relationship
Age Survived Negative correlation (older passengers less likely to survive)
Sex Survived Female passengers more likely to survive
Pclass Survived Higher-class passengers more likely to survive

Patterns and Trends

  • Most passengers embarked from Southampton
  • Majority of passengers were in third-class
  • Survival rate decreases with age
